
Campus Buildings and Facilities Report (Comprehensive Version)

1. Ramanujacharya Bhavan (Classroom & Administrative Building)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
This is the main classroom block and also the administrative center of the college. It houses academic classrooms, the offices of the Principal and Vice Principal, and essential student service facilities.

Entrance:
- Main entrance located on the North side of the building.

Facilities:
- Stairs: Centrally located, connecting all floors and the basement.
- RO Water Outlets: Installed in the South-West corner of the basement.
- Basement: Contains a Chemistry Laboratory and Staff Room for Teachers.
- Lift: Not specified.

Washrooms:
- On every floor:
  - Boys' Washroom: North-East side
  - Girls' Washroom: North-West side

Administrative & Student Services:
- College Office:
  Located on the Ground Floor (South side) — this is the central office for all administrative and student-related services.

  Lost ID Card Procedure:
  1. Report the loss immediately at the College Office.
  2. Fill out an ID Reissue Form and pay the replacement fee at the Accounts Section (within the same office).
  3. Collect your new ID card from the office within 2–3 working days.

- Lost and Found:
  The College Office also manages the Lost and Found section.
  Students can report or claim lost belongings (like books, wallets, stationery, keys, etc.) during office hours.
  Unclaimed items are recorded and securely stored for a limited period.

Other Rooms and Departments:
- Ground Floor:
  - Principal's Room - South side
  - Vice Principal's Room - South side
  - Office (Administrative & Accounts) - South side
  - Dean Office - South-West side
  - Board Room - South-West side
  - Boardroom & Autonomous Cell - South-West side
  - Exam Control Room - North-East side

- First Floor:
  - Department of Information Science and Engineering (ISE) - South-West
  - Department of Physics - North-West
  - Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) - North-West

- Second Floor:
  - Department of Mathematics - North-West

- Third Floor:
  - Department of Mechanical Engineering - South-West
  - Staff Room - North-West


2. Madhavacharya Bhavan (Library Building)
-------------------------------------------
Purpose:
This building serves as the central library and includes multiple classrooms. It is a quiet academic zone accessible to all students and staff.

Entrance:
- Main entrance located on the North side.

Facilities:
- Stairs: Located at the center of the building, connecting to both first floor and basement.
- Basement: Contains Staff Room for Teachers and Washrooms.
- Lift: Not specified.
- Reading Rooms: Available for individual and group study.

Washrooms:
- Girls' Washroom: Beside the Library (North side).
- Boys' Washroom: On the East side.
- Basement: Contains both Girls' and Boys' Washrooms on the North side.

Other Rooms and Sections:
- Library: Situated on the North side of the building.
- Classrooms: MB1, MB2, MB3, MB4, MB5 - each accommodating around 60 students.
- Staff Room: Located in the basement.


3. Shankaracharya Bhavan (Lab Building)
----------------------------------------
Purpose:
Known as the Lab Building, this block contains specialized labs and departmental rooms for technical programs such as CSE, ISE, MCA, and AIML.

Entrance:
- Main entrance located on the North side, accessible from the campus road.

Facilities:
- Lift: Situated on the West side.
- Stairs: Located on the South side, connecting all floors.
- Water Dispensers: Available at the South-East corner.
- Stationery Shop: Positioned on the South side, near the stairs.
- Coffee Shop: Located on the West side of the ground floor.

Washrooms:
- Ground Floor:
  - Boys' Washroom - South-West side
  - Girls' Washroom - South-East side

Other Rooms and Departments:
- Ground Floor:
  - Infirmary for first aid - North-West side
  - TPO Office/T&P Cells - North-West side
  - Training & Placement Cell - North-West side
  - Admission Cell - West side
  - Coffee Shop - West side
  - Seminar Hall - Central area
  - Auditorium - East side
  - Stationery Shop - South side (near stairs)

- First Floor:
  - AIML Labs 1–4
  - Department of MCA - West side
  - MCA Labs 1–3
  - Department of ISE and IT Cell - West side
  - Department of CSE Staff Room 1 - South-East side

- Second Floor:
  - Department of CSE Staff Room 2 - North side
  - Gents Staff Room - West side
  - CSE Labs 1–8
    - CSE Lab 6 also serves as the CAED Lab

- Third Floor:
  - Department of ISE Staff Room 2 - North side
  - IS Labs 1–8

Additional Notes:
- Access: Only students and faculty of technical departments are allowed in lab areas.
- Wi-Fi: High-speed campus Wi-Fi available on all floors.
- Safety: All labs are equipped with fire extinguishers and safety charts.

Staff Room Directory:
- CSE Staff Room 1: Shankaracharya Bhavan, First Floor (South-East side)
- CSE Staff Room 2: Shankaracharya Bhavan, Second Floor (North side)
- ISE Staff Room 1: Shankaracharya Bhavan, First Floor (West side)
- ISE Staff Room 2: Shankaracharya Bhavan, Third Floor (North side)
- Mechanical Staff Room: Ramanujacharya Bhavan, Third Floor (North-West side)
- General Staff Room: Ramanujacharya Bhavan, Basement


4. Boys Hostel
----------------
Purpose:
Accommodation facility for male students of the college, providing residential amenities and student support services.

Location:
Located in the northern part of the campus, providing easy access to academic buildings.

Facilities:
- Common rooms for recreation and study
- Mess facility providing meals
- Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the building
- Laundry facilities
- 24/7 security and warden supervision

Access:

- Visitor entry requires prior permission and registration
