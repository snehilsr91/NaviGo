1. Ramanujacharya Bhavan (Classroom & Administrative Building)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
This is the main classroom block and also the administrative center of the college. It houses academic classrooms, the offices of the Principal and Vice Principal, and essential student service facilities.

Entrance:
- Main entrance located on the North side of the building.

Facilities:
- Stairs: Centrally located, connecting all floors and the basement.
- RO Water Outlets: Installed in the South-West corner of the basement.
- Basement: Contains a Chemistry Laboratory and Staff Room for Teachers.
- Lift: Not specified.

Washrooms:
- On every floor:
  - Boys' Washroom: North-East side
  - Girls' Washroom: North-West side

2. Madhavacharya Bhavan (Library Building)
-------------------------------------------
Purpose:
This building serves as the central library and includes multiple classrooms. It is a quiet academic zone accessible to all students and staff.

Entrance:
- Main entrance located on the North side.

Facilities:
- Stairs: Located at the center of the building, connecting to both first floor and basement.
- Basement: Contains Staff Room for Teachers and Washrooms.
- Lift: Not specified.
- Reading Rooms: Available for individual and group study.

Washrooms:
- Girls' Washroom: Beside the Library (North side).
- Boys' Washroom: On the East side.
- Basement: Contains both Girls' and Boys' Washrooms on the North side.

Other Rooms and Sections:
- Library: Situated on the North side of the building.
- Classrooms: MB1, MB2, MB3, MB4, MB5 - each accommodating around 60 students.
- Staff Room: Located in the basement.

4. Boys Hostel
----------------
Purpose:
Accommodation facility for male students of the college, providing residential amenities and student support services.

Location:
Located in the northern part of the campus, providing easy access to academic buildings.

Facilities:
- Common rooms for recreation and study
- Mess facility providing meals
- Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the building
- Laundry facilities
- 24/7 security and warden supervision

Access:
- Entry restricted to registered male students
- Visitor entry requires prior permission and registration

6. Food Court (College Mess)
---------------
Purpose:
Central dining facility serving a variety of cuisines and food options for students and staff. It is the combined mess for both Girls' Hostel and Boys' Hostel.

Location:
Located near the main entrance of the campus for easy access during meal times. It also has a bakery outside it.

Facilities:
- Multiple food counters offering different cuisines
- Seating area for dining
- Clean and hygienic food preparation areas
- Affordable pricing for students
- Students from south campus hostels can have their meals here as well
- Day scholars can pay 50rs and have a meal here too

Operating Hours:
- Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM
- Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
- Snacks: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

10. Parking
-------------
Purpose:
Designated parking area for students, staff, and visitors. Combined parking for all vehicles.

Location:
Strategically located near the main entrance for easy access. Bike parking shed is located near the area between Shankaracharya Bhavan and Ramanujacharya Bhavan.

Facilities:
- Combined parking for all vehicles
- Separate sections for two-wheelers and four-wheelers
- Security surveillance
- Covered parking for two-wheelers
- Visitor parking area
- Bike parking shed

Rules:
- Valid parking permit required
- Follow designated parking spaces
- No overnight parking without permission

Location:
